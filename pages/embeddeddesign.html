Embedded Dev.

I have experience in embedded system design and development. I’ve worked with Arduino boards for prototyping, as well as ESP32 and STM32 microcontrollers. For circuit and PCB design, I’ve used tools like Proteus, Multisim, and KiCad. I’m proficient in programming 8051 microcontrollers using Keil software, and STM32 ARM controllers with STM32CubeIDE. I’m familiar with FreeRTOS for real-time systems, and I have a good understanding of Raspberry Pi.

In terms of programming, I have expertise in C, Embedded C, and Python, specifically for embedded systems. For version control, I use Git with GitHub. Additionally, I’ve worked with Node-RED for IoT applications and have experience programming the 8085 microprocessor, ATMEGA328P, 8051, and STM32 microcontroller families. I also incorporate 3D modeling to aid in prototyping.
